About us

As the nation’s No. 1 real estate firm, Brickell Lane Realtors has guided people home since 1968. We’re a full-service brokerage firm, offering residential and commercial real estate, mortgage, settlement, insurance, property management, corporate relocation and vacation rental services.

A focus on exceptional service to clients and support for our real estate agents means being tech-savvy and digitally connected, keeps our agents on the cutting edge.

But that’s just our business; our success is defined by being so much more. We are a network of thousands of agents and industry professionals united in a passion for real estate. We are facilitators connecting people with places to call home. And most importantly, in the communities where we live and work, we are trusted insiders, friends and neighbors.
